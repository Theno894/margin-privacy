01

Overview

Margin ("the App") is a multilingual vocabulary learning application developed by Susie Hao. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only what is necessary to provide the service, and we never sell your data to third parties.

02

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — Your Apple ID email address and display name, provided when you sign in with Apple.
  • Vocabulary data — Words, phrases, sentences, and notes you save within the App.
  • Usage preferences — Your selected source and target languages, app language settings.
  • Profile data — Optional avatar image and display name you choose to set.
  • Subscription status — Whether you are a free or Pro subscriber, managed via RevenueCat.
03

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve the App experience:

  • To authenticate your identity and maintain your account securely.
  • To store and sync your vocabulary cards across your devices.
  • To power AI-generated definitions and translations via the Gemini API.
  • To manage your subscription and unlock Pro features.
  • To remember your language preferences between sessions.
04

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — Secure cloud database and authentication. Your vocabulary data is stored on Supabase servers with Row Level Security enabled, meaning only you can access your data.
  • Google Gemini API — AI language model used to generate word definitions, translations, and example sentences. Queries are sent to Google's servers and are subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management platform. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple; RevenueCat only receives anonymised subscription status.
  • Apple Sign In — Authentication provider. We receive only the minimum information Apple provides (email and name).
05

Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored in Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled. This means database policies enforce that each user can only read, write, update, or delete their own data — even at the infrastructure level.

We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Your Apple authentication token is never stored on our servers.

06

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you choose to delete your account, all associated vocabulary data and profile information will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

To request account deletion, please contact us at the email address below.

07

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

08

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting the App.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
